We B3 Exports engaged in exporting Mats which made of a special kind of grass called "Korai". The Korai Mat weaving craft is a customary skill of the people in southern part of Tamil Nadu, India.

This is a traditional craft and being done by the people of the villages. Korai mats are formed from natural fibre resource collected from korai grass which are amphibious grown locally for the purpose of manufacturing mat.
